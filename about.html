About

Harmony Experience is a project born in 2022 for the course “Informatica Umanistica”, in the University of Bologna. My name is Lorenzo Cioni, I’m a 21 years old guy and I’m a communication student.

One of the things I love most is expressing myself creatively, and that's why I wanted to make a place for people to live their own creative journey, by 1) learning about creativity, 2) listening to the products of creativity, and finally 3) being creative firsthand, through a space for expressing themselves.

As you may've noticed, this particular journey revolves mainly around music, and is thought especially for music lovers and aspiring songwriters. However feel free to use this space to express yourself as you like.

As for the name "Harmony experience", well, I wanted to give my users a multisensorial experience, involving three of the five senses (sight, hearing and touch). And, as the senses do, the three main pages of the site are intended to work together in “harmony”, even though they are used one at a time. I hope I succeded in that, and that you’ll enjoy your permanence on the site.
