Useful resources

Here are some tools that might make your writing process more easy:

  1. Wordreference - A simple dictionary to find definitions, synonyms, and also words translated into different languages
  2. Rhymezone and Double-rhyme - Dictionaries to find rhymes
  3. BrainyQuote - A site to read inspirational quotes from famous authors, or about a certain topic
  4. Voicerecorder.io - An online voice recorder
  5. Musicca - A site for playing instruments online, and learn music
  6. Bonus: Musescore - After writing your lyrics, if you also want to write your own music, you can use this software for creating music sheets. It's also available on Android and IOS

Do you wanna read some theory about song structure and find tips to write better? Go to the page See the music
